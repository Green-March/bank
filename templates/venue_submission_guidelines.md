# Source and Compliance Guidelines
Last Updated: YYYY-MM-DD

## Approved Source Priority
1. Official disclosure: EDINET
2. Official market/provider APIs: J-Quants / JPX
3. Company IR materials
4. Reputable secondary media (for context only)

## Data Handling Rules
- Keep raw download artifacts for traceability
- Store retrieval timestamp and endpoint
- Do not overwrite prior raw files without reason
- Keep normalized schema stable across runs

## Reporting Rules
- Distinguish fact vs inference explicitly
- Provide source references for key claims
- Separate historical data from forecasts
- Always include risks and uncertainty
